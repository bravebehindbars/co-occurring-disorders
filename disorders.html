Dual Diagnosis:

Dual diagnosis refers to the coexistence of substance abuse and mental health disorders...

  • Roughly 50 percent of individuals with severe mental disorders are affected by substance abuse.
  • 37 percent of alcohol abusers and 53 percent of drug abusers also have at least one serious mental illness.
  • Of all people diagnosed as mentally ill, 29 percent abuse alcohol or drugs.

    Mental health problems and substance use disorders sometimes occur together...
